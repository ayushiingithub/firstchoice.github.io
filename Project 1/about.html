An FTC rule requires sellers to ship items as they promised in their ads. If a seller doesn’t promise a time, it has to ship your order within 30 days after it gets your name, address, and payment, or permission to charge your account. Many sites offer tracking options, so you can see exactly where your purchase is and estimate when you’ll get it. If you pay by credit card buy don’t get the item, you can dispute the charge. The privacy policy should let you know what personal information the site is collecting, why it’s being collected, and how it’ll be used. If you can't find a privacy policy, can’t understand it, or don’t like how the site will use your information, consider going to a different site.
